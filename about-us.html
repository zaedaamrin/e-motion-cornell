Our Mission


E.Motion is a performance group that performs K-pop dance covers. All those w ho are interested in dancing or K-pop are welcome. We hope to showcase our passion for K-pop songs and dances through our performances to the Cornell community and provide opportunities for dancers to challenge themselves.
