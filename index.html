WHO ARE WE

A dance group at Cornell that focuses on teaching members of all dance backgrounds and preparing them for performances.

Our club provides opportunities for dancers to challenge themselves. We regularly host workshops, that are open to the general public, and have audition-free group dances, as well as organize audition-only performance teams.

Join us in dance or catch us at our performances, like our annual Spring Showcase or events such as the Mid-Autumn Festival, and more.

We offer:

  • Dance Workshops - Learn the choreo to your favorite song!
  • Audition-Free Group Dances
  • Audition-Only Performance Teams

OUR ACCOMPLISHMENTS

We have performed at many major Cornell events as well as at KCON New York, Stage K Cover Challenge and have won global dance cover contests hosted by 1theK.

E.Motion was voted Cultural Contributor at the 2018 CDA Awards and best Video Editing at the 2019 Awards.
